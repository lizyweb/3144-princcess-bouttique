ABOUT US

Princcess Bouttique

Princcess Bouttique, established in 2003 by A. Stella, MDFD Fashion Designer, specializes in stitching bridal blouses, salwar suits, lehengas, and more. Renowned for its precision and creativity, the boutique has become a trusted name for customized designs, reflecting a blend of tradition and modernity. It’s a hub for elegant, handcrafted fashion.

Motivated by a passion for empowering young talents, Princcess Bouttique extended its vision by starting a fashion and tailoring institute. Beyond offering stylish and affordable clothing for every occasion, it serves as a one-stop destination where creativity thrives, providing trendy outfits and fostering a love for fashion in the community.
